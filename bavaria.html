  • The most titled club in Germany and one of the most titled clubs in the world.

Bayern

Bayern is the most titled professional football club in Germany, with 33 championship titles and 20 German Cups, and 6 victories in the Champions League. At the end of 2013, the team became the first German club to win the Club World Championship.

Until 1925, Bayern played in one of Germany's oldest football venues, the city's so-called first stand; then for many years it shared the municipal stadium with the Munich 1860 club, after which the stadium was popularly called “Sechziger” (“Sixtieth”). It was almost completely destroyed during the Second World War, a lot of effort was spent on reconstruction, but still the stadium was opened on time and accommodated 44 thousand people.

In 2002, together with Munich 1860, a new stadium, the Allianz Arena, was built, where the team plays all its matches. The arena is illuminated in different colors depending on the colors of the club that plays there. The arena is lit red when Bayern are playing; blue - when Munich 1860 is playing; white - when the German national team plays. Initially, the arena was co-owned by both clubs, but then Bayern acquired the share of Munich 1860 and became the sole owner of the stadium.

  • In May 2012, Bayern opened a museum of the club's history on the territory of the Allianz Arena.
  • Bayern took 3rd place in the list of the best football clubs of the 20th century according to FIFA, 2nd place according to Kicker magazine. IFFHS also ranked Bayern in 3rd place in the ranking of the best European clubs of the 20th century.
  • Bayern was originally created as a separate club.
  • Incredibly, the first twenty-five years turned out to be a failure, the team was unlucky for a long time, and only in the 1924-1925 season did Bayern begin to have its first successes.
  • The most expensive player is Jamal Musiala (€ 110 million).

The 2023/2024 season is the 59th season for the Bayern Munich football club in the German championship and the 124th since the founding of the team.
